Georgios Theodoropoulos is currently a Chair Professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at [SUSTech](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/en/) in Shenzhen. He has previously served as an Executive Director at [Durham University](https://www.dur.ac.uk) and held positions at [IBM Research](https://research.ibm.com/labs/ireland), [Trinity College Dublin](https://www.scss.tcd.ie/), the [University of Birmingham](http://www.bham.ac.uk/), and [NTU Singapore](http://www.ntu.edu.sg/SCE/).

He is a member of the [European Academy of Sciences and Arts](https://euro-acad.eu/) and a Fellow of the [World Academy of Art and Science](http://www.worldacademy.org/). He has board director accreditations from institutes in [Singapore](https://www.sid.org.sg/) and [Hong Kong](https://www.hkiod.com/) and holds a certificate from the [INSEAD International Directors Programme](https://www.insead.edu/executive-education/corporate-governance/international-directors-programme).

Georgios Theodoropoulos is a Chartered Engineer and holds a PhD in Computer Science from the [University of Manchester](http://www.cs.man.ac.uk), where he researched in [S. Furber](http://apt.cs.manchester.ac.uk/people/sfurber/)'s [AMULET/APT](http://apt.cs.manchester.ac.uk/) Group.
